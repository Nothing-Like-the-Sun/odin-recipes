Butter Cream Frosting

Description

Butter Cream Frosting for cakes, cupcakes, or cinnamon rolls

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2-3 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1-2 tablespoons milk (as needed)
  1. Add the “slightly softened” Butter (somewhere in between straight out of the refrigerator and room temperature) to the mixing bowl along with the Vanilla Extract.
  2. Mix the Butter and Vanilla together until they are fully combined (about 2 minutes on medium speed.)
  3. Add powdered sugar, mixing on low until butter and sugar are combined.
  4. Add milk to desired consistency
  5. Increase mixer speed to high and mix for 4 minutes
